I finally was able to talk with Ysabet. She intimated to me that she had urgent discussions she wanted to have with me, and was happy I was there. We made plans to meet more discretely after the wake, though we decided to accelerate the schedule after comparing a few notes.

Ysabet had been investigating the fire and discovered Hands of Liberty uniform remnants and badges in the wreckage. That, with things not being right about Amanta supposedly opposing the construction, led her to investigate everything round town and the family.

Many irregularities were found in Mother Tirtsa's books. She had a sizable set of large obligations to various banks in Condrol. She was overextended and the banks (as political pawns) had been attempting to leverage her, though she did not give in.

There was also word that the "army has found the gods", referring to the Stag Lady and Lord. That is to say, Condrol is influencing the military in the pass!

Oh, and a Hands of Liberty squad is coming, likely drawn by OneTusk's fireworks.

What is a wiki? [ edit ]

A wiki is a web-based community tool that allows multiple users to edit the pages.

How do I get an account? [ edit ]

You contact one of the admins of the wiki and they will give you an account.

How do I edit pages? [ edit ]

Short answer: Click the edit tab, change the text, preview it, save the page. That's it!

The long version: Editing Basics for Mediawiki

If you want to mess around and try some stuff out, please do it in the Sandbox rather than on a live page.
